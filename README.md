## What is a 4-Channel Video to Fiber Installation Kit?

A 4-channel video to fiber kit converts analog video signals from four CCTV cameras into optical signals and transmits them over a single fiber optic line. This fiber-based approach reduces signal degradation, eliminates electromagnetic interference, and supports longer distances than traditional copper cabling. Versitron’s fiber kits are compatible with standard camera formats and engineered for professional system deployment.
